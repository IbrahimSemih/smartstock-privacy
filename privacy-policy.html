Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartStock ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application SmartStock.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password (if using Supabase authentication)
  • Product Data: Product names, descriptions, prices, stock quantities, images, barcodes
  • Inventory Data: Stock entries, stock history, categories, brands
  • Profile Information: Display name, phone number, company name (optional)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage data and preferences
  • Device information (for app functionality)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the app functionality
  • Managing your inventory and product data
  • Authenticating your account (if using Supabase)
  • Improving our services
  • Sending you notifications about low stock (if enabled)

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using SQLite database. This includes all product information, stock records, categories, and brands.

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you choose to use Supabase authentication and cloud sync:

  • Your account information (email, password) is stored securely on Supabase servers
  • Data synchronization is optional and under your control
  • Supabase complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Supabase (if used) for authentication and cloud services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Local data is encrypted using SQLite security features
  • Cloud data is protected by Supabase's security infrastructure
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Export your data in PDF or Excel format
  • Opt-out: Disable cloud sync and use the app offline

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
